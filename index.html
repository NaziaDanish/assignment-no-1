About Saylani Welfare

Saylani Welfare Trust is a renowned non-profit organization dedicated to serving humanity by providing essential services to the underprivileged. It was established with the vision of eradicating poverty and uplifting communities through various welfare programs. Saylani offers free food distribution, ensuring that thousands of people receive daily meals. The organization also focuses on education, providing scholarships, vocational training, and IT courses to empower individuals with skills. Additionally, Saylani runs healthcare services, offering free medical checkups, treatments, and medications to those in need. Their clean water project ensures access to safe drinking water in remote areas. Saylani also supports widows, orphans, and disaster-affected people through financial aid and rehabilitation programs. The organization plays a key role in interest-free micro-financing, helping small businesses grow. Their blood bank and ambulance services save countless lives every year.
